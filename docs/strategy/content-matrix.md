# Jukkai content matrix (Phase 3 skeleton)

**Phase 3 deliverable, produced July 2, 2026 and reconciled July 10 alongside
`docs/strategy/sitemap.md`.** The left half is now a provisional IA candidate, not a
frozen build contract. `docs/strategy/foundation.md` v1.7 is truth;
`docs/strategy/messaging.md` binds registers, proof themes, vocabulary, and every
[VALIDATION-NEEDED] item. Nothing in this file is final page copy.

## How to read this file

- **Table A** records the current IA candidate. Page grouping, homepage H1, B2B
  visibility, cost-guide priority, and contact structure remain subject to the IA /
  contact confidence reviews.
- **Table B** is the proof/migration/validation half, keyed by the same Page
  names.
- **The provisional project inventory** catalogues every old-site project page as
  a candidate proof row. Existing public projects are presumptively reusable;
  Crystelle's final selection and any new privacy or asset constraint still override.
- Legacy decision keys (`S1`, `C1..C9`, `L1..L6`, `V1..V8`) survive in some Phase 3
  rows for traceability. The named inputs in `questions-for-crystelle.md` and
  messaging §9 are now authoritative; do not infer priority from the old numbering.
- Validation status vocabulary:
  - `draft-ready`: Phase 4 may draft and freeze copy (claims canon applies).
  - `draft-gated`: Phase 4 may draft with placeholders; copy freeze blocked on
    the referenced decision(s).
  - `review-gated`: IA/SEO confidence review must choose the structural model.
  - `research-gated`: contact/conversion evidence must choose the mechanism.
  - `proof-pending`: structure and copy rails ready; final slate/facts remain open.
  - `reserved`: post-launch; do not build at launch, keep the row.

   cost guide, B2B page, homepage hierarchy, and contact path are structural
   hypotheses. Galerie copy and modular case-study content may draft without freezing
   those seams.

   defect (messaging §10.10).

   magasin, ecommerce, catalogue, product-grid, or price-on-request codes in a draft
   are defects. `Galerie` is canonical; its surrounding language makes it warm and
   distinctively Jukkai. The thesis line appears once, Galerie page only.

   contextual 2012 studio-founding story. The eco label is real supporting proof.
   Bridge claims stay vision-tense until a documented placement exists.
5. **Quote hygiene**: any French verbatim reused from `docs/research/audience.md` must be
   spot-checked at source; testimonials need documented permission (C4).
6. **Not in this matrix but launch-weight**: GBP + citations checklist, page-by-
   page 301 map (built from Table B's Old URL column + the inventory), GA4/Search
   Console with multi-channel contact-intent measurement, external profile updates. Phase 4
   owns their specs (analytics, schema, redirect map) as separate artifacts.
7. **Schema (Phase 4 decisions flagged here)**: LocalBusiness sitewide;
   ArtGallery-equivalent on `/galerie/` (category fluency without importing cold
   gallery codes); FAQPage on the three acquisition surfaces.
8. **Geolocated SERP spot-check** on the two launch clusters before the SEO spec
